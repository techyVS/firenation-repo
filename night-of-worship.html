Night of Worship

Worship known as SHACHAH in Hebrew means to bow down or to fall down flat. It’s that moment we humble ourselves before YAHWEH. Worship is a divine moment of total reference to God. It’s that moment we withdraw completely from everything about self but absolute focus on Yahweh. When we allow Yahweh’s Spirit have unhindered flow through our spirit-man, our lives changes from inside-out. It’s no doubt that the spiritual controls the physical.

NIGHT OF WORSHIP (NOW) is that moment we worship God in SPIRIT and TRUTH as seen in John 4: 23-24 23 But the hour cometh, and now is, when the true worshippers shall worship the Father in spirit and in truth: for the Father seeketh such to worship him. 24 God is a Spirit: and they that worship him must worship him in spirit and in truth.
