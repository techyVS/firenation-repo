Doing good project (DGP)

We believe strongly in following the steps of our master and savior, Jesus Christ.

Acts 10:38 38 How God anointed Jesus of Nazareth with the Holy Ghost and with power: who went about doing good, and healing all that were oppressed of the devil; for God was with him.

The Doing Good Project (DGP) is an arm of FireNation Ministries that focuses on ministering help to men & women who never had children to look after them at old age. This category of elderly people mostly from 60 years up, most are neglected or forgotten. We place them on our mostly feeding program and in some cases; we train them on skills that can also empower them, fund the enterprise and put smiles on their faces thereby letting them know that if the society forgets them, God will never forget them. The second group of individuals we minister help to, are prostitutes. We present the message of salvation to prostitutes and then further train them in a profitable skill/enterprise as a better option for livelihood after they must have based through proper discipleship.

Matthew 25:35-40 35 For I was an hungred, and ye gave me meat: I was thirsty, and ye gave me drink: I was a stranger, and ye took me in: 36 Naked, and ye clothed me: I was sick, and ye visited me: I was in prison, and ye came unto me. 37 Then shall the righteous answer him, saying, Lord, when saw we thee an hungred, and fed thee? or thirsty, and gave thee drink? 38 When saw we thee a stranger, and took thee in? or naked, and clothed thee? 39 Or when saw we thee sick, or in prison, and came unto thee? 40 And the King shall answer and say unto them, Verily I say unto you, Inasmuch as ye have done it unto one of the least of these my brethren, ye have done it unto me.
