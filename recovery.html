Recovery Conference (RC)

This is a deliverance program for individuals who have been afflicted with the spirits of addictions and dangerous habits. Everything in life is controlled by spirits: there is a spirit responsible for wisdom, spirit of wickedness, spirit of excellence, spirit of goodness. You can either be under the influence of the spirit of God or the spirit of demons.

The good news is, no matter how long you have been under the influence of negative habits, the chains of the habits and addictions can be broken by the saving grace of Jesus Christ.

Romans 6:19 I speak after the manner of men because of the infirmity of your flesh: for as ye have yielded your members servants to uncleanness and to iniquity unto iniquity; even so now yield your members servants to righteousness unto holiness.

The Demonic STRATEGY!
Almost everyone struggling with one form of addiction or the other, the devil introduced the seed at a moment you were down or broken in spirit and then he presented a real and convincing reason why you should indulge. The act gradually will become a way of coping with what caused you pain, helping you mask the situation. Now, you may not know that the first action was actually a seed planted by the enemy. The devil does this softly so that you may not notice his strategy. He will continue to push you to repeat the act over and over until it has been registered in your sub-conscious mind and enters on auto-pilot. At this moment, the victim ends up in a destructive cycle of bondage to sin, rendering him/her incapable of experiencing the FREEDOM and wholeness he/she can truly experience.

We have helped hundreds of individuals addicted to pornography, masturbation, smoking, drugs, etc come out victorious from the chains of demonic addictions, thereby turning their slavery from uncleanness to slavery to righteousness.
